Life

I was born and raised in the heart of Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal. My family moved to the suburbs when I was around seven. I went to a military boarding school at the age of nine and graduated tenth standard at sixteen. I finished my GCE A Level from St. Xavier's College, Maitighar.

Currently, I am pursuing an undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering at Tufts University, MA. I am involved in the on campus film crew TUTV and the campus mewspaper The Tufts Daily. I am also a regular pickup soccer player, and spend a lot of my time at the Cage.
